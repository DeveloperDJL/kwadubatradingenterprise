About Us

Finding Inspiration in Every Turn

In 2010, Kwaduba Trading Enterprise was established as an African-owned company
with the aim of venturing into various service provider markets such as construction,
road building, school nutrition, mining supply, plant hiring, transportation, distribution,
waste rock treatment, and crushing/screening, as well as high-pressure cleaning services.

Vision

Kwaduba Trading is led by professional businessman with a mission to grow the
business by expanding to other provinces, While Creating Job Opportunities especially
involving women and the youth.


Mission

Kwaduba Trading is dedicated to build life long relationships with its clients through
professional and excellent product delivery with honesty, respecting all
cultures it is serving and wants to be recognized as the leading provider
within a 100km radius.
